Accordion

My interest for coding first started out a couple of years ago when I was watching an interview of Bill Gates where he was describing how he started his career. Somewhere in it I heard him say that he “Started writing software when…”. That left me with the question, “How does someone write software?”. So I started googling and found myself at codeacademy.com. I was doing that on and off for a couple years before finally diving back in about 6 months ago.
I live in the San Francisco Bay Area but I am also mobile and happy to take on clients outside of California
Although its dependent upon the project, I aim to typically have everything completed within a month of agreement.
It ranges from people who have never created a website before and want someone to take full charge, to someone who has had a website for years and wants to spruce things up a bit.
I am 26 years old.
